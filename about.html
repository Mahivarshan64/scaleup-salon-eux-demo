Who We Are

Beauty Born from Passion

You & Me Salon was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: to create a space where artistry and warmth come together. What started as a small studio has grown into one of the city's most beloved beauty destinations.

We believe that beauty is deeply personal. Every service we offer is tailored to reflect your unique style and personality. Our team stays at the forefront of trends while honouring classic techniques that stand the test of time.
